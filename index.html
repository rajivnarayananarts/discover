About Me

Rajiv Malayil, a distinguished dental technician at Calicut, India, for the last 41 years, has seamlessly transitioned from crafting smiles to creating visual symphonies on canvas.

Discover the incredible journey of this self-taught artist as he unveils his paintings for online sale in this store.

Starting his artistic voyage at the age of 59 during the COVID pandemic, Rajiv's creative impulse blossomed, proving that art knows no age barrier. With an unyielding passion for painting, Rajiv mastered the art form entirely on his own, showcasing the limitless possibilities of self-discovery and artistic expression.

His exhibits in this store are a testament to his dedication and evolving proficiency in the world of visual art. This store unveils not just a collection of paintings but a milestone in Rajiv's artistic journey that invites you to witness the essence of his creative spirit.
